K-TECH Vision & Mission

Partnership

In order to achieve critical mission objectives and realize the organization vision, K-TECH pursued a strategic affiliation with a distinctive and well reputed international educational institute. After a thorough investigation and study, Kaplan University was chosen to be K-TECH’s international affiliate. Kaplan University was chosen because of the many similarities between the two partners which can be outlined as follows:

  • Kaplan University provides a learning experience that is career oriented and supported by state-of-the-art technology based delivery methods.
  • At the undergraduate level, Kaplan University's main focus is the employment of its graduates (the rate of employment is 90% within 6 months of graduation).
  • Kaplan University offers programs in both technology and business fields.
  • Kaplan University highly appreciates the importance of providing flexible learning options for the community, i.e. associate, bachelor and master’s degrees; and part-time, full-time.
  • Kaplan University soundly addresses the market needs for ready-for the-job graduates by offering curricula that are continuously revised and updated to cater for the latest trends and changes in technology.

Kaplan University is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission (HCL) and is a member of the North Central Association (NCA), a recognized accreditation association listed by the US Department of Education.

Another advantage of affiliating with Kaplan is the agreement to offer K-TECH graduates the opportunity to continue their education towards a bachelor degree at Kaplan based on a 2+2 model. K-TECH graduates can continue their education at any of Kaplan campuses or with Technical Feasibility Study Kaplan online and receive a bachelor degree after additional two years (full time based). The Articulation Agreement between K-TECH and Kaplan will insure K-TECH graduates can transfer of 90% of their credits to Kaplan University toward a bachelor degree in the field of interest.
