K-TECH Careers


Working With Us

Welcome to our careers page at Kuwait Technical College (K-Tech)

We are tremendously proud to be acknowledged for the hard work to improve our services in the higher education sector at the heart of the community.

The HR Department at K-TECH regulates the employment procedures and imposes approved personnel policies in providing equal employment opportunities for all applicants through formal candidate selection by considering the required skills, experience, capabilities, and through personal interviews as well as taking necessary tests, if applicable. All under applicable labor laws of the State of Kuwait.

To Apply, submit the following documents via email to recruitment@k-tech.edu.kw.

  1. Letter of interest addressing background and experience relevant to the position.
  2. An updated Curriculum Vita.
  3. List of three professional references and their contact information.
  4. Experience and salary certificates.
  5. Course Expertise and Justification - Specify the courses from academic catalogue for which you would like to be considered.
  6. Copy of Attested Academic Degree(s) and Transcripts.
  7. Copy of Passport.
  8. Other documents that are helpful for your application. (Optional)

We appreciate all applications received; however, only those candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.

K-Tech is an equal opportunity employer and welcomes applications from all qualified candidates.
