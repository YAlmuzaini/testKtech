K-TECH Vision & Mission

Strategies

K-TECH strategy which will be strictly followed in all initial programs and any possible future improvements or additions is to offer 2-Yr academic associate degree that is:

  • Occupational and career oriented
  • Delivers mid-term value information
  • Technically, state-of-the-art, in all fields of study
  • Articulated into a 4-Yr bachelor degree

Competitive Advantages

K-TECH will offer the following competitive advantages:

  • International Affiliation

    • Strong Academic Model
    • Best practices in education
    • Quality control and assurance

  • Flexible degree programs

    • Class schedules designed for full-time or part-time students
    • Morning, afternoon, evening courses
    • Convenient lab hours

  • Learning for workplace

    • Instructors with real-world experience and international qualifications
    • Focus on work-related applications
    • Lab sessions and activities incorporated into curriculum
    • Programs constantly updated with input from employers and industry

  • Strategic Campus site

    • Convenient location
    • Comfortable classroom and study environments

  • Excellent resources and support to meet student needs

    • High-Tech Campus (Laptop Education, E-Learning, Wireless Networking)
    • Academic advisors
    • Library resource center (e-library, virtual library)
    • Tutors

  • Ongoing Career advising; internships; and career search support for students, graduates and alumni

  • Deliver quality education and all related services using state-of the-art technology and computing solutions that include:

    • Student's use of mobile computers for in class and outside class activities
    • Using high speed wired and wireless network to provide the benefits of campus-wide, ad hoc and mobile connectivity to all computer resources
