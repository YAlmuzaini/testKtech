K-TECH Library

As Library of an academic institute represents the heart of the campus, it is dedicated to maintain excellent services in providing information services and resources to support the information need of its community. The students in the institution have to actively engage in the process of Information seeking for assignments, research and decision making. The primary mission of the library is to support educational and research activities of the institute by providing physical and intellectual access to information which keeps up-to-date with the latest trends and development in their specific fields of interests. It is essential for a student to know how to find, evaluate and apply needed information, which requires information literacy skills.

K-TECH Library Overview

Kuwait Technical College library provides a pleasant atmosphere of Information seeking techniques in the spacious library hall segregated for girls and boys. We actively support the discovery, creation and preservation of knowledge. As a new library, we emphasize on developing collections and services that support and encourage the academic activities of faculty, students and staff. The library collections and information services primarily support K-Tech graduate programs and construct search techniques. Convenient Wi-Fi service is available throughout the learning resource center hall. The Library is located on the second floor of the college and provides work-stations for group discussions and study room facilities, as well as well suitably furnished carrels for individual study.

Library Services:
  • Computer lab provides access to the campus network, Wi-Fi and assistance in device connectivity and technical support
  • Expert level support and services of acquiring information resources and reference services
  • Provide the resources and space of learning in a collaborative and innovative way
  • Offer study room facilities and support to meet the demands of learning and teaching
  • Keeping in touch with the new trends in the library and information world
